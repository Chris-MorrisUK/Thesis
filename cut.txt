The COMPASS system comprises a number of subsystems. At the highest level they may be split into the an in cab compotant and a number of aditional subsystems with in the RBC. These systems can be further sub devided: the incab system is infact 2 systems, a trackside system that relays the information and the incab system which, using it's driver machine inferface, dsiplays signal aspects, or marker boards as required, to the driver. The systems housed within the RBC include Diverse Monitor and Communicator (here on DMC) a siemens train control product and the railway core ontologies.
